Label Encoding

How it works: Assigns a unique integer to each category (0, 1, 2, ...).

Pros: Simple, memory efficient, works with all algorithms.

Cons: Implies ordinal relationship that may not exist.

Best for: Tree-based algorithms, ordinal categories.
