What is LoRA?

Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) is an efficient fine-tuning technique that adapts large pre-trained models without modifying their original weights.

Instead of updating the massive frozen weights (W), LoRA introduces two small matrices (A & B) that represent the adaptation as a low-rank decomposition.
