What is PEFT?

Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) enables adaptation of large language models to specific tasks by training only a small fraction of their parameters.

Instead of updating all parameters (expensive and slow), PEFT methods like LoRA add small trainable adapters while keeping the base model frozen.
