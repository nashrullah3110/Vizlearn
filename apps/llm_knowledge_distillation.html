Knowledge Distillation is a technique where a large, complex "Teacher" model transfers its knowledge to a smaller, more efficient "Student" model.

The Teacher provides soft labels (probability distributions) that contain richer information than simple hard labels, helping the Student learn more effectively.

Deployment: Faster inference with minimal accuracy loss
